 Loan Utilization Tracking via Mobile Application

Project Title

Loan Utilization Tracking via Mobile Application-A mobile-based application designed to help beneficiaries track the utilization of loan funds, maintain digital expense records, upload proof documents, and enable banks or agencies to monitor fund usage efficiently. The system improves transparency, accountability, and verification of loan-related expenses.

Problem Statement

Many individuals obtain loans for education, agriculture, business, and personal purposes. However, after the loan amount is disbursed, there is no effective mechanism to monitor whether the funds are used for the intended purpose. Manual record-keeping methods are prone to errors, lack transparency, and make verification difficult. This can result in fund misuse and increased monitoring effort for lending institutions. Therefore, a digital solution is needed to track expenses, manage proofs, and ensure proper utilization of loan funds.

Project Objectives

* Monitor and track the utilization of loan funds.
* Maintain digital records of all loan-related expenses.
* Improve transparency between beneficiaries and lending institutions.
* Allow users to upload receipts and supporting documents.
* Generate reports and summaries of loan utilization.
* Reduce paperwork and manual record maintenance.
* Securely store user, loan, and expense information.
* Send reminders for pending proof submissions.
* Enable remote monitoring and verification by banks and agencies.

Module List

1. User Management Module

Manages user registration, login, authentication, and profile details.

2. Loan Management Module

Stores and manages loan information such as loan amount, purpose, type, and status.

3. Expense Tracking Module

Records expenses made using loan funds and tracks utilization progress.

4. Proof Upload Module

Allows users to upload receipts, invoices, and supporting documents for verification.

5. Report Generation Module

Generates utilization reports, expenditure summaries, and balance information.

6. Reminder & Notification Module

Sends alerts and reminders regarding pending submissions and updates.

7. Monitoring & Verification Module

Enables banks and agencies to review records, verify proofs, and monitor loan usage remotely.

Expected Outcome

The system will provide a secure and centralized platform for managing loan utilization. Beneficiaries will be able to record expenses, upload supporting documents, and monitor loan spending in real time. Banks and agencies will be able to verify fund usage remotely, generate reports, reduce manual effort, improve transparency, and ensure that loan funds are utilized for their intended purpose.
